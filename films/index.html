There are 14 films in the Star Trek franchise, dating back to the first motion picture in 1979.

  • The Original Cast – The first 6 films take place in the 22nd century and feature the original cast, including William Shatner as James T. Kirk and Leonard Nimoy as Spock.
  • The Next Generation – The next 4 films take place in the 23nd century, starring the Next Generation cast, including Patrick Stewart as Jean-Luc Picard and Brent Spiner as Data.
  • Kelvin Timeline – There are 3 films that take place in the Kelvin Timeline's 22nd century. These films feature Chris Pine as James T. Kirk and Zachary Quinto as Spock.
  • Standalone – 1 straight-to-streaming film stars Michelle Yeoh as Philippa Georgiou. Her character originates from Star Trek: Discovery, but the film is a separate, standalone story.
