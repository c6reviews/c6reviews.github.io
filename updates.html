Site Update Acknowledgements

Currently, updates on this site are primarily focused on content, with a secondary focus on features and accessibility for desktop browsers. I am aware that this site doesn't really look good on smaller screens, such as tablets or phones. Some day, maybe after most of the content is done, maybe then I'll work on making the site more mobile-friendly. Until then... enjoy these content updates!

April 24, 2026 1.6
  • The Star Trek: Lower Decks Series Guide is now online!
  • The Borg Topic Guide is also live!
  • Site performance improvements:
    • All images have been converted from PNG to WEBP, and many large episode images were reduced in size by 40%.
      As a result, the site's total size has decreased by over 1 GB.
    • All fonts have been converted from TTF to WOFF2.
      I finally realized why some text was taking so long to display on some pages: the font file was humongous.
  • Spun up an Umami instance on Railway to gather page visit statistics since everyone is probably rejecting the cookies needed for Google Analytics (and I don't blame you one bit!)
Other fixes and quality improvements
Added a TOC and a “conventions used on this site” section on the About page, in case anyone questions why I don't capitalize “easter” when talking about easter eggs; fixed the never-appearing scroll-to-top button on the About page so that it... appears; made some consistency updates to the tables in the Seven of Nine, Q, and Borg guides; made a few aesthetic improvements on the Q topic guide; the borders on season and guide tiles on the series home pages now also highlight when hovering; aria-label attributes added to images in the SVG overlay on the comprehensive timeline for better WCAG compliance.

First Contact Day!
 1.5.2
  • The film review for Star Trek: First Contact has been posted!
    And yes, it was done weeks ago, but I postponed releasing it until First Contact Day, because it seemed like the right thing to do 😂

Other fixes and quality improvements
Aesthetic improvements were made to timelines (including a higher-quality image of Cochrane and the Phoenix); the full timeline spoiler menu now shows the number of spoilers hidden for each series; slightly improved the appearance of the “pips” icons; sanitized title and timeline search inputs so I don't get “Little Bobby Tables'd”; changed a whole bunch of absolute and relative links to be root-relative; moved styles for the cookie consent popup to the css file where they belong instead of the js file; made consistent the mentions of guest stars reprising their roles; and put a considerable amount of work into making consistent the italicization of series names, film names, and ship names across the website... I probably missed a few, but at least I wasn't dealing with the hyphens again.

  • The home page now has a Quote of the Day that rotates through a pool of 31 quotes, plus some special quotes that show up on certain days.

  • Citations for songs sung by the Doctor in Star Trek: Voyager were added to the appropriate episodes.
Other fixes and quality improvements
Starfleet Academy tile was added to the home page; a few aesthetic fixes and improvements to the universe timeline were made, including a notification flag on the spoiler menu button when any spoilers are being shown; improved the appearance of the season/guide tiles on each series page; added the new cookie consent form and the "reset cookie preferences" link at the bottom of each page; random review box re-roll button now removes displayed episodes from the selection pool to ensure a new one each time, and pictures from series with wide aspect ratios have been zoomed and cropped for better visibility; some more source pop-ups were added to the timeline; and though it pains me greatly, I removed the colons from all mentions of Star Trek Nemesis since the title officially has no colon.
