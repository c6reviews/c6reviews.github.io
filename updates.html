Site Update Acknowledgements

Currently, updates on this site are primarily focused on content, with a secondary focus on features and accessibility for desktop browsers. I am aware that this site doesn't really look good on smaller screens, such as tablets or phones. Some day, maybe after most of the content is done, maybe then I'll work on making the site more mobile-friendly. Until then... enjoy these content updates!

February 13, 2026 1.5.1
  • Site cookies now let you save some spoiler preferences (currently on the main timeline and the main spoiler toggle for Star Trek: Picard).
  • The home page now has a Quote of the Day that rotates through a pool of 31 quotes, plus some special quotes that show up on certain days.
  • Improvements to the Random Review box on the home page including the addition of the episode's Watch Recommendation.
  • Citations for songs sung by the Doctor in Star Trek: Voyager were added to the appropriate episodes.
Other fixes and quality improvements
Starfleet Academy tile was added to the home page; a few aesthetic fixes and improvements to the universe timeline were made, including a notification flag on the spoiler menu button when any spoilers are being shown; improved the appearance of the season/guide tiles on each series page; added the new cookie consent form and the "reset cookie preferences" link at the bottom of each page; random review box re-roll button now removes displayed episodes from the selection pool to ensure a new one each time, and pictures from series with wide aspect ratios have been zoomed and cropped for better visibility; some more source pop-ups were added to the timeline; and though it pains me greatly, I removed the colons from all mentions of Star Trek Nemesis since the title officially has no colon.

Other fixes and quality improvements
Added a “re-roll” button to the Random Review box on the home page; lots of small HTML fixes throughout the site not worth detailing; fixed an issue on the Picard reviews where list items would not display correctly when showing spoilers for a single episode; added a statement in the footer of every page about this being a fan site not endorsed by Paramount, even though I have delusions of grandeur and would love for Paramount to send me a ‘cease and desist’ letter because that would mean my site was actually popular; finally settled on standardizing to the British style of placing punctuation outside of quotation marks when the punctuation is not necessarily part of the quote, and fixed a bunch of instances where I hadn't done that – I may be American, myself, but I think the Brits got this one right – and apparently I hadn't punished myself enough with the hyphens. Also, Star Trek: Starfleet Academy was added to the Star Trek Universe Full Timeline on Jan 16th.
